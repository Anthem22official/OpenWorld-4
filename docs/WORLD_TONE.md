# OpenWorld — World Tone

⚠️ **HARD RULES**

**Age & Romance:** Protagonist = adult only. Romantic/sexual characters = adults only. Minors = NPCs only, zero romance, zero violatable content.

**Sexual Orientation:** Characters ≠ all gay. Mix straight, bi, gay, ace. Realistic world has mixed orientations. Protagonist is gay; supporting cast is diverse.

**Checkpoint:** Approved for development baseline. All generated content must follow this document.

---

## Core Concept

Near-future (2040) metropolitan gay romance sim. Focus: adult protagonist from small town navigating big city ambition, wealth, attraction, social climbing. Not nostalgia. Not trauma lit. Pure urban momentum.

**Visual mood:** Glass towers, neon, sea views, smart transit, nightlife, hierarchy. Day = work. Night = desire.

**Core emotion:** Excitement + culture shock + seduction by the city.

---

## Protagonist

Adult male, small-town origin. Wants: better looks, money, status. Attracted to mature/fit/accomplished men. Driven by work, social scenes, fitness, nightlife. Reshaped by city.

---

## Romance

Built on attraction first: looks, body, status, voice, lifestyle. Can stay shallow or deepen. First impression → ambiguity → comparison → chosen.

**Avoid:** Everyone likes protagonist. Trauma as personality. Samey pacing.

---

## Characters

**Criteria:** Clear look/body/style/status. Trackable schedule. Desire & position. Conflict potential. Supports ≥1 story.

**Generate:** Body first (appearance, role, appeal) → schedule second.

**Avoid:** No charm, only trauma, same location cluster.

---

## Locations

Every location = ≥1 function: display status, meet/flirt, work/train, show hierarchy, generate events.

**Avoid:** Pretty but hollow. Old/nostalgic.

---

## Events

Happen naturally from people + place + status. Short, bright, tempting. Ambiguity, comparison, invitation.

**Avoid:** All-trauma, all-heavy.

---

## Taboos

- Rewrite tone
- Age down protagonist or romanticize minors
- Swap to straight/harem
- Make city old/fantasy/apocalypse
- All-trauma characterization
- Dilute body/status/desire/consumption

---

## Visuals

Semi-realistic, K-pop-adjacent. Adults, clear faces, defined bodies, status clothing. Backgrounds: no people, near-future urban, high contrast.
